Planning an event? Make it one to remember with a professional photo album! 
We know you have put a lot of effort into organizing your gathering, and want to partner with you in making memories.
We offer pre-event planning to make sure we capture all the important moments, as well as post-event material reviews to allow you to curate your album before you release it. 

We are pleased to offer our services for 

 - Public Events
 - Private Gatherings
 - Corporate Events
 - Music Festivals
 - Live Action Role-Playing Events (LARPs)
 - Parties of any size

Please Note: We are not currently booking weddings, except for "B-roll" (i.e. no primary photography).
