Dan is an avid outdoor adventurer with many years of expertise in climbing, white water kayaking, mountaineering, backcountry skiing, and caving, as well as experience downhill mountain biking, open water diving, canyoneering, sky diving, jumping off bridges, and street luge. 
Having learned many lessons "the hard way", Dan has been an instructor and trip leader with MIT's Outting Club (MITOC) since 2010, sharing his knowledge and experience by teaching students a wide range of outdoors skills and activities. 
Far from being a potential liability, Dan can join your group as a self-supported memeber, a consultant, or a collaborator. 
If you want photos of your inheriently dangerous activities in remote locations, Dan can get there with you to make sure it looks epic (regardless of the outcome)!
